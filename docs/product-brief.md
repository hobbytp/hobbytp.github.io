## 1. Executive Summary

This document defines the product vision and strategic direction for "Peng Tan's AI Blog" - a comprehensive Hugo-based AI technology blog focused on delivering high-quality content about artificial intelligence research, technologies, and industry insights.

## 2. Project Vision & Purpose

### 2.1 Vision Statement
To become a leading Chinese-language AI knowledge hub that bridges cutting-edge academic research with practical industry applications, making AI knowledge accessible and actionable for both technical and business audiences.

### 2.2 Mission
- **Educate:** Provide clear, insightful analysis of AI papers and technologies
- **Inform:** Deliver daily AI industry news and updates
- **Bridge:** Connect academic research with real-world applications
- **Inspire:** Showcase innovative projects and industry leader insights

### 2.3 Core Value Proposition
- **Depth:** In-depth analysis beyond surface-level news coverage
- **Breadth:** Comprehensive coverage across AI domains (papers, tech, projects, companies)
- **Timeliness:** Daily automated news collection with human curation
- **Accessibility:** Chinese primary content with English translations for key articles
- **Practicality:** Focus on actionable insights and real-world applications

## 3. Target Audience

### 3.1 Primary Audiences

**AI Researchers & Students (40%)**
- Graduate students in AI/ML fields
- Academic researchers
- PhD candidates
- *Needs:* Latest paper insights, technical deep dives, research trends

**AI Engineers & Developers (35%)**
- Software engineers working with AI
- ML engineers
- Technical leads
- *Needs:* Implementation guides, tool reviews, best practices

**Tech Leaders & Product Managers (20%)**
- Engineering managers
- AI product managers
- CTOs and tech executives
- *Needs:* Industry trends, strategic insights, competitive analysis

**AI Enthusiasts & Learners (5%)**
- Self-learners in AI
- Career changers
- Technology enthusiasts
- *Needs:* Foundational knowledge, learning paths, industry overview

### 3.2 Geographic Focus
- **Primary:** Mainland China and Chinese-speaking regions
- **Secondary:** Global Chinese diaspora and international AI community

## 6. Competitive Analysis

### 6.1 Competitive Landscape
**Direct Competitors:**
- Medium AI publications (Chinese)
- Technical blogs from AI companies
- Academic researcher blogs

**Indirect Competitors:**
- AI news aggregation sites
- Online learning platforms
- Technical documentation sites

### 6.2 Competitive Advantages
- **Comprehensive Coverage:** Broader than single-focus blogs
- **Daily Automation:** More frequent than manual curation
- **Chinese Language:** Primary focus on Chinese market
- **Technical Depth:** Deeper analysis than news sites
- **Multi-Format:** Articles, tutorials, news, and analysis
